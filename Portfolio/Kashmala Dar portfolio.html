Your Questions Answered

What is your design process?

My design process begins with understanding your vision and goals. I then move to conceptualization, followed by digital design and refinement, ensuring the final product perfectly matches your needs.

What are your specialties?

I specialize in Resume designs, Presentations, Business and Invitation cards, Kids worksheets, Educational posters and charts, Banner and profile visuals, Menu Cards, Social media posts and YouTube thumbnails. My expertise spans across various industries, helping businesses establish a strong visual presence.

What tools do you use?

I typically use Adobe Creative Suite, including Photoshop and Illustrator. 

What is your turnaround time?

My turnaround time varies depending on the project's complexity and scope. I always provide a realistic timeline after our initial consultation.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes, I offer revisions to ensure your complete satisfaction. We'll discuss the revision process during our project kickoff.
